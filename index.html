How to Play

  • ❥ To walk right, hit the RIGHT arrow key.
  • ❥ To walk left, hit the LEFT arrow key.
  • ❥ To jump, hit the UP arrow key.
  • ❥ To attack, hit the SPACE key.

Description

In Cat About Town, you play as a housecat that's escaped her home to run around and cause mischief!

Trot around, jump on things, and catch some critters! When you attack you cause some damage, and when your prey's health is depleted you get a snack!

Images

  • ❥ Brown Bird: You can attack her if you jump up to get her first!

  • ❥ Red Bird: You can get to him by first jumping up onto the branch, then jumping up off of it to attack!

  • ❥ Rat: This little guy is the easiest to catch! He's on the ground, served up on a silver platter! YUM!
